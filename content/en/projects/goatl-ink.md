## About the Project

goatl.ink is a modern platform for creating personal landing pages that lets users shape their
online presence quickly and easily. The platform offers a user-friendly interface for creating
individual pages with various content and links.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Landing Pages**: Create individual pages with your own branding
- **Link Management**: Central management of all important links and social media
- **OIDC Authentication**: Secure login via OpenID Connect
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized display on all devices
- **Fast Delivery**: Hosting via Cloudflare for maximum globally distributed performance

## Technology Stack

The platform is built on modern web technologies:

- **Frontend**: Nuxt.js, Vue.js 3, TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for modern, responsive design
- **Infrastructure**: Cloudflare Workers and Cloudflare D1 for fast global delivery
- **Authentication**: OpenID Connect (OIDC) for secure user management

## Use Cases

- Content creators and influencers consolidating their social media presence
- Freelancers and the self-employed seeking professional online business cards
- Small businesses wanting simple company profiles
- Personal portfolios and projects
